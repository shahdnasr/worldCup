QATAR and The World Cup 2022

Organizing the World Cup by Qatar

On December 2, 2010, FIFA announced that Qatar would be awared the honor of hosting the 2022 FIFA World Cup ; thus, the most popular competition in the world will be organized in the Middle East for the first time in the 92-year history of the tournament.

The Qatar file, which won the honor of organizing, was distinguished by providing the "best organized" for the World Cup ever, al all the stadiums proposed to organizethe tournament are located only 60km from each other, expect for once one stadium.

Infrastructure Qatar's Strategy

Upgrading the infrastructure Qatar's strategy to host the World Cup includes continous infrastructure development; This will not only enable visitors and residents to enjoy the tournament, but will also lay the foundation for future growth and national development.

Countries Which Take Part in the World CUP 2022

The State of Qatar will host 31 other countries during the tournament. Compete in 64 matches that open and final in Lusil's premier stadium, which was built specifically for the World Cup, and contains a carbon-neutral footprint (carbon -neutral).
